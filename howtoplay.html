How to Play

Welcome to the Animal Matching Game! To play:

  • Choose one animal category you want to play
  • Click on any card to flip it and reveal an animal.
  • Click on another card to find its matching animal.
  • If the animals on both cards match, they will stay flipped.
  • If the animals do not match, both cards will flip back after a short delay.
  • Your goal is to match all the animals on the board.

Have fun exploring the animal kingdom!
